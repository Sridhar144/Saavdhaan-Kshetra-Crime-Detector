About

Find Out More About Us

Welcome to आरोग्यMarg! At आरोग्यMarg, our mission is to harness the power of cutting-edge technology to combat Neglected Tropical Diseases (NTDs) and connect patients with the care they need. We are committed to making a tangible difference in the lives of individuals affected by NTDs by providing a platform that combines early disease detection, education, and easy access to healthcare resources.
